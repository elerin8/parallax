Sunny days around the world

Sun gives you vitamin D

The temperature of the sun varies from around 27 million degrees Fahrenheit (15 million degrees Celsius) at the core to only about 10,000 degrees F (5,500 degrees C) at the surface.

Colorful sunsets

Colors of the sky

Clouds that are higher in the atmosphere make the sunsets more colorful, clouds that are lower in the atmosphere tend to mean that there is a storm occurring.

By the time you see the sunset, it is already gone.

Fun facts about sunsets

Sunsets are more radiant after a rainstorm. Seasons can affect the sunsets. “The Golden Hour” is more vibrant in deserts and islands.
